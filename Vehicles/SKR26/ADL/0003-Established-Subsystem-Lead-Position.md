## Context and Problem Statement
Each component used and action taken to build the vehicle will be categorized into a subsystem in order to divide the work and responsibility amongst the team.
How will subsystem responsibility be split up?

## Decision Drivers

* Balanced work and responsibility.
* Flexibility.
* Consider other teams' organizational structure.

## Considered Options

* Subsystem leads
* Co-Leads
* Component/Module leads

## Decision Outcome

Chosen option: Use *Subsystem Lead* position and allow subsystem leads to appoint *Component Leads* as needed.
Rejected: Co-Leads, responsibility in the hands of multiple contradicts the main function of subsystem leads.

### Consequences

* Good, work and responsibility is well balanced
* Good, because it allows for flexibility via Component leads
