## Context and Problem Statement

We would like to name this vehicle. Currently the name is SKR26.
## Decision Drivers

* Representative of solar themes
* Representative of racing themes
* Representative of UCF themes
* Create a sense of character for the vehicle
* Unique both in the literal sense (no other cars exist with the same name) and in the figurative sense (not too cliche)
## Considered Options
* **SKR**
* **Pegasus**
* **Northstar**
* **Polaris**
* **Opportunity**
* **Legacy**
* **Knightmobile**
* **Solaris**

* Suffix YY e.g. 26

## Decision Outcome

Chosen option: **Solaris** based on popular vote
Rejected: Suffix YY based how the majority of teams name their vehicles
### Confirmation

Implementation: All official naming of this initial vehicle thus far and from now on will go by Solaris
