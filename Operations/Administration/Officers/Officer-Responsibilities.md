## All officers
All officers must uphold the standards of the club. They must also hold their fellow officers to those same standards.
All officers must fill out their section in the meeting minutes with the topics they will discuss during their time along with an estimated duration. This needs to be completed before the meeting.
## President
The president is the primary public representation of the club. The president is responsible for communicating with the SGA, the OSI and our faculty advisor.
The president is solely responsible for the re-registration of the club [per OSI requirements](https://osi.ucf.edu/rso/#reregistering-an-rso).
The president must help organize and assist with officer duties
The president is one of the three financial signers.
## Vice-President
The primary responsibility of the Vice-President is to assist the president with all endeavors.
The Vice-President must schedule general body meetings.
Audit of financial transaction
Organize lodging and transportation
assist in special projects
The Vice-President one of the three financial signers.
## Treasurer / Business Director
The Treasurer must keep track of all funds and expenses that go through the club. This must accumulate into an audit-able financial history for the club.
The Treasurer is in charge of generating budget and spending reports monthly.
The Treasurer must maintain a Bill of Materials for each vehicle with the help of subsystem leads.
The Treasurer must collect and track member dues.
The Treasurer must provide necessary financial documentation to maintain non-profit status.
The Treasurer is one of the three financial signers.
## Secretary / Director of Data Acquisition
The secretary must announce meetings. The secretary is responsible for ensuring that meeting minutes are complete, making meeting presentations, and completing meeting summaries.
## Parliamentarian
The parliamentarian must assist the president in maintaining RSO status.
The parliamentarian must advise officers and members of parliamentary procedure.
## Reporter / Outreach Lead
The Reporter must manage the club's social media presence.
The reporter is in charge of communicating with partners.
The Reporter is responsible for organizing fundraising events.
## Technical Director
The Technical Director is responsible for the rules compliance of the entire car.
The Technical Director oversees subsystem integration.
The Technical Director should act as a mediator in the event of a disagreement between subsystems.
## Safety Officer
The Safety Officer must be registered and present at all events that require a designated safety officer.
The Safety Officer must maintain their CPR certification.
## Subsystem Lead
Each Subsystem Lead is responsible for successful operation of their respective subsystem.
Each Subsystem Lead should act as a mediator in the event of a disagreement within their respective subsystem.
Each Subsystem Lead is responsible for tracking the progress of their respective subsystem as well as identifying situations that are blocking progress. Both of these must be reported to the secretary before each meeting.
